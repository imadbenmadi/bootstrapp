Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CloudSync

What is CloudSync and how does it work?

CloudSync is a comprehensive collaboration platform that helps teams work together more efficiently. It integrates project management, communication, and analytics tools into one powerful platform. Simply sign up, create your workspace, invite your team, and start collaborating instantly.

Is my data secure on CloudSync?

Yes, security is our top priority. We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.2+ for data in transit. CloudSync is compliant with GDPR, SOC 2, and HIPAA standards. We undergo regular security audits and penetration testing.

Can I integrate CloudSync with other tools?

Absolutely! CloudSync offers integrations with popular tools like Slack, GitHub, Jira, Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams, and many more. We also provide a comprehensive REST API for custom integrations.

What support options are available?

We offer multiple support channels: email support for all plans, chat support for Professional plan and above, and dedicated account managers for Enterprise customers. Our average response time is under 2 hours.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, there are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll have access until the end of your billing period.
